Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Crafted with Love

Founded in the vibrant heart of Kampala, Savannah Spice celebrates Uganda's rich culinary heritage. Our mission is simple: share authentic flavor with hospitality and heart.

Every dish tells a story, from the fertile soils of the Rwenzori region to the energy of Owino market. We source locally, cook traditionally, and serve with pride.

Join us for an elevated cultural dining experience where every meal is a celebration.
